My Agenda

About plans

I am planing to apply at the CGL (http://www.colognegamelab.de/) which is the very opportunity to apply when one is interested in games or game related work.
There are specialization tracks for your application and during the course of the study, including game design, game programming and game arts.

Concerning my CGL application I have the preference to game arts due to my skillset and huge interests in concept art. In this specialization everything appeals to me due to my artastic affinity.

Global Game Jam 2016

Assets for the game

For the first time in my life I participated in a Global Game Jam in 2016 at the CGL and, also my first time, created art for a digital game.
It was an extraordinary expirience concerning the things I learned about making games and about creating art for games.
So you can find the game which is called "Coloritum" here http://globalgamejam.org/2016/games/coloritum and also my teammembers who did all the programming, level design and game logic.
